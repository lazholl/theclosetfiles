Ennead

en·ne·ad

/'ene,ad/

noun

●1. a group or set of nine.

ENNEAD, a group of nine, started as a collection of nine basic pieces for the wardrobe that offer different styles of t-shirts that are more feminine and classic than the athletic or too-trendy styles found on the market. With clients all over the US, they had to source the most functional and travel friendly pieces while not sacrificing the style. Their quest as stylists to find the easiest pieces for their client’s closets led them to create ENNEAD, a collection of t-shirts and lounge wear that can be worn throughout all aspects of one’s day. The collection is soft and easy-to-care for fabrics in custom dyed colors that are muted and classic. All nine core styles can easily work for the same client, dressed up or down, for any age or climate. Jordan and Meg continue to create new styles every season based on the needs of their clients and the lack in the market, as Ennead was created to be worn as ones uniform.
