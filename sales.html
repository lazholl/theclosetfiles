Pop-up Sales

After noticing a shift in the retail climate and their clients' desire to be more environmentally friendly, Jordan and Meg started hosting pop-up sales of designer resale. Through these bi-yearly sales, they are hoping to help drive the trend of choosing gently used, high end designed, and couture clothing and accessories as an environmentally conscious fashion choice.
