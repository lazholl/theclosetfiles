About

After more than 15 years in retail honing their craft, best friends Meg Chapman and Jordan LaValle decided to also become business partners and start The Closet Files, a private, referral based, full service operation that specializes in all aspects of styling and organizing one’s wardrobe.

From shopping and packing to categorizing and re-selling, Meg and Jordan work as partners bringing two different perspectives and points of view to styling and options of how to dress – in order to ultimately cleanse the closet, invest wisely, and achieve a desired look.

Their goal is to create an easy daily interaction with one’s wardrobe, where all options are visible and to help one feel great about getting dressed each day, whether it be business or pleasure. You will be steered in the right direction in terms of investing in the right pieces for you and re-coup some money spent on pieces that remain unworn or not age appropriate.

Meg and Jordan’s current roster consists of both private and celebrity clients in Los Angeles, Palo Alto, Hawaii, New York, Chicago and the Cayman Islands.

Jordan

Jordan LaValle’s style is an ode to her Californian bohemian roots, classic, but in an unexpected way – feminine, vibrant, and spirited. Besides being an entrepreneur, she is a wife, mother of 4 and avid cook and baker. After graduating High School at the age of 16, Jordan LaValle simultaneously pursued a career in music while taking culinary courses at California State University, Northridge. After years of singing, songwriting and touring, Jordan got her feet wet in the fashion industry at Lisa Kline on LA’s renowned Robertson Blvd. Throughout the years, Jordan’s experiences’ have grown to include serving as a buyer and manager of Tracey Ross on Sunset Blvd., head of styling and VIP client and studio services for Vince, and a manager and stylist for Lanvin in Malibu. Jordan currently resides in Southern California.

Meg

Meg Chapman’s style is an ode to the easy and minimal. Her style can be described as somewhere between a tomboy and the French girl aesthetic. Besides being an empowered entrepreneur, she is a wife, mother of 2 girls, and certified Pilates instructor. Graduating from Philadelphia University with a Bachelor’s degree in Apparel Management and Marketing and an MBA, Meg Chapman started her career working at Nicole Miller In Philadelphia and Richmond, VA for 6 years. After that, she advised students for one year at the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising in San Diego. Ultimately missing the interaction with clients, Meg returned to the retail world working with the luxury powerhouses Prada and Lanvin for a total of 10 years, eventually overseeing the entire West Coast Retail operation including buying and VIP client services for Lanvin. Meg currently resides in Southern California.
